4. Key Metrics & Insights
Overall Performance:

Total Sales Amount: 438K
Average Order Value: 121K
Total Profit: 37K
Total Quantity Sold: 5,615 units

Regional Performance:

Maharashtra leads with the highest sales contribution
Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh follow as strong markets
Delhi and Rajasthan show smaller but consistent sales volumes

Temporal Analysis:

January contributes 24% of total monthly profit (highest)
February accounts for 21%
November and March contribute 25% and 19% respectively
April shows the lowest contribution at 10%
December shows strong year-end performance with notable profit spike

Product Category Performance:

Printers generate the highest quantity sold
Bookcases rank second in sales volume
Saree, Accessories, and Tables complete the top 5 categories

Payment Preferences:

COD (Cash on Delivery) dominates at 44% of all transactions
UPI follows at 21%
Debit Card accounts for 13%
Credit Card at 12%
EMI represents 10% of payment methods

5. Dashboard Features

Quarter-wise Filtering: Toggle between Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 for seasonal analysis
Interactive Visuals: Cross-filtering enabled across all charts for dynamic exploration
Multi-dimensional Analysis: Simultaneous view of geographical, temporal, product, and payment data
Profit Tracking: Month-over-month profit comparison with visual bar charts
City-wise Sales Distribution: Horizontal bar chart showing state-level performance ranking

6. Business Applications
This dashboard is intended for use by:

Sales managers tracking regional performance and setting territory goals
Finance teams monitoring profitability trends and payment method efficiency
Inventory planners analyzing product demand patterns
Executive leadership making strategic decisions based on comprehensive sales intelligence

7. Key Takeaways
The Vedanta Sales Dashboard reveals that Maharashtra is the dominant market, January drives peak profitability, and COD remains the preferred payment method. The product mix is diverse with printers leading in volume, while the dashboard's interactive capabilities enable users to drill down into specific quarters, regions, or categories for granular insights.
